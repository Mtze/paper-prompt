## Submission Category

**System and Tool Paper** (selected category):

> System and tool papers present a software system or other tool of value in a computing
> education setting. They will be evaluated based on the potential contribution of the system
> to the community, the effectiveness of the comparison to and differentiation from existing
> similar systems, and the quality of the evaluation and/or discussion of the design of the tool.
> We expect contributions in this category to present tools that have a certain degree of
> maturity and matching empirical evaluation!

## Review Criteria (Tool Paper Category)

1. Potential contribution of the system to the community
2. Effectiveness of comparison to and differentiation from existing similar systems
3. Quality of the evaluation and/or discussion of the design of the tool
4. Maturity evidence (deployment, adoption, usage data)

## Scope and Topics

Koli Calling 2025 accepts submissions in computing education. Relevant topics for this paper:

- Development, use, and evaluation of tools to support computing education
- Teaching approaches and assessment in computing education (Assessment Phase)
- Learning analytics and educational data mining (future work direction)
- Distance, online, and blended learning of computing topics
